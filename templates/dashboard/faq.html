FAQ

Something's not working with the address lookup

Make sure to check for typos. Make sure Street, Avenue, Drive, Lane, etc. are spelled out. Abbreviations will not work!

The information for past addresses isn't being saved!

Make sure you click the 'SAVE' button at the bottom of the page before you search for a new address!

How do the arrow buttons work and what are they for?

Their main use is so that you can move to the next/previous house without typing in the number again. They will add/subtract 2 to the current house number. If the house number is "weird", they will add/subtract 2 to the last number. So for example 31-28N will become 31-30N. You still need to press the "Search" button.

What do the lettered tags next to each past visit mean?

They're just a shorthand for the information you put in during that visit.

  • Knocked
  • Door Opened
  • Owners Available
  • X - Owners not interested in selling
Do the owners appear in any order?

Nope!

Why do some addresses not have owner info?

This tool is used only for residential properties. Furthermore, NYC does not require landlords to register if the property has 1-2 residential units and the owner or their immediate family lives there. In either of those cases, the owner will not show up in the database.

Are there any plans to expand this tool to work for addresses outside of NYC?

Unfortunately not. The app works by using the NYC Open Data and the owner search funtionality would not work withouot this.
